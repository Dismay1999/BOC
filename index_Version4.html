About Brothers of Charity Services Ireland

Brothers of Charity Services Ireland is a national organisation that provides supports to people with intellectual disabilities and their families across the country.

Inspired by our ethos, we strive to promote dignity, respect and equal citizenship for every person we support.

Learn more about our history, ethos and organisational structure

Our mission

We are an inclusive, progressive organisation committed to the people we support and their right to lead full and valued lives as equal citizens.

We work in partnership with individuals, families, communities and statutory bodies to create supports that are led by the person’s own goals and choices.
